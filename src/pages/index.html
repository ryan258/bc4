The Wibe Has Hit the Streets!

For those who are not familiar with the term “Wibe,” it’s basically a Bahamian term that was coined by my generation which refers to specific situations that impacted change or were a transition to a new stage.

It’s a new way of thinking. It was a term that was vague enough that it can be just about anything but it was also specific enough to comprehend the subject matter. Each song on the album explores the Wibes that I have went through in my life at one point or another.
