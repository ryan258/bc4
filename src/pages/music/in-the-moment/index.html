All my life I considered myself to be a loner.

Not by choice at first but I knew I was different, but I couldn’t explain it.

I would try to fit in but somehow it seemed the more I try the more epic the fail.

So I was a loner and over time it became my normal.

In fact I would say I enjoyed being in my own space better than being with people. I connected with my true self and it felt right.

Don’t get me wrong, I love people.

I also enjoy making people feel happy and great about themselves.

But every so often I must unplug from the social dimension and enter my own truth.

Everyone should enjoy some alone time. I believe its healthy to always live a balanced life because there is no greater treasure than peace of mind.
